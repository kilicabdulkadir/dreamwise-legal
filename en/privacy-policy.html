Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2025

At DreamWise, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

When using our application, we may collect the following data:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and profile picture
  • Dream Entries: Dream content and notes you enter
  • Voice Inputs: Audio data when using voice dream recording (processed only for transcription)
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics and preferences
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Provide dream interpretation services
  • Deliver personalized experiences
  • Improve app performance
  • Provide technical support
  • Fulfill legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security updates
  • Access control and authorization

4. Third-Party Services

Our application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics / Mixpanel: For usage analytics
  • Google AdMob: For ad delivery
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • Sentry: For error tracking

These services have their own privacy policies and may process your data according to their policies.

5. Advertising and Personalization

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Your advertising identifier may be used to deliver ads relevant to your interests. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymous statistical data may be retained
  • Some data may be kept longer due to legal requirements

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Request data portability

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through in-app notifications or email.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@dreamwise.app
