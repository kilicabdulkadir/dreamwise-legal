Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cancel my Premium subscription?

You can cancel your subscription from your device settings. For iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. For Android: Google Play Store > Subscriptions.

Are my dream entries safe?

Yes! All your data is encrypted and stored on secure servers. Only you can access your dream entries.

How does voice recording work?

You can describe your dream using voice. Your recording is transcribed by AI and saved to your dream journal. The audio file is deleted after processing.

How can I delete my account?

You can use the "Delete Account" option in app settings or email support@dreamwise.app. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

How accurate are dream interpretations?

Our dream interpretations are based on traditional dream symbolism and modern psychological approaches. However, these are for entertainment and personal exploration only and do not replace professional psychological counseling.

Can I use the app offline?

You can record your dreams offline. However, AI interpretations and synchronization require an internet connection.

When Submitting a Support Request

To help us assist you faster, please include:

  • Your device and operating system version
  • App version (Settings > About)
  • Detailed description of your issue
  • Screenshots if available
